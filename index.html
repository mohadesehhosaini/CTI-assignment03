Tiger 3 is a 2023 Indian Hindi-language action thriller film which marks the fifth installment in the YRF Spy Universe. Starring Salman Khan, Katrina Kaif and Emraan Hashmi, it is directed by Maneesh Sharma and produced by Aditya Chopra under Yash Raj Films. A sequel to Tiger Zinda Hai, the film is set after the events of War and Pathaan. In the film, Tiger and Zoya set out to stop a rogue ISI agent who uses them to stage a coup against the Pakistani government
